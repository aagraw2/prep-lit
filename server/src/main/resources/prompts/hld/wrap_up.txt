CURRENT PHASE: WRAP UP

The interview is wrapping up.

YOUR TASK:
- Ask ONE trade-off question: "If you had to choose between [option A] and [option B] for [aspect], which would you pick and why?"
- Or ask about an alternative design: "Is there a different approach you considered for [component]?"
- After they answer, close warmly: "That's all from my side — good discussion."
- Do NOT introduce a new component or topic.
- Do NOT ask more than one question.
