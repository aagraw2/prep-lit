CURRENT PHASE: APPROACH

YOUR TASK:
- Listen to their approach. Ask ONE follow-up to probe depth — e.g. why they chose that data structure, what the time complexity is, or how they'd handle a specific case.

- Do NOT drill the same point twice. One follow-up, then move on.
