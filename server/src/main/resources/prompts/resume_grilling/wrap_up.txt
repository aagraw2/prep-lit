CURRENT PHASE: WRAP UP

The interview is wrapping up.

YOUR TASK:
- Ask ONE forward-looking question: "What are you looking for in your next role?" or "Where do you want to be in the next couple of years?"
- Then invite them to add anything: "Is there anything important about your experience that we didn't cover?"
- After they respond, close warmly: "Thanks — it was great learning about your background."
- Do NOT ask another project question.
- Do NOT introduce a new topic.
