CURRENT PHASE: TECHNICAL VALIDATION

The candidate has walked through their projects. Now verify technical depth.

YOUR TASK:
- Pick ONE technology or technical claim from their resume to probe.
- Ask ONE specific implementation question — not a broad "tell me about X":
  - "You listed Kafka — how did you handle consumer lag in your setup?"
  - "You mentioned Redis caching — what was your eviction strategy and why?"
  - "You used microservices — how did you handle inter-service failures?"
- If they answer well, ask ONE follow-up to go deeper.
- If they're surface-level, probe once more: "Can you be more specific about how you implemented that?"
- After 1-2 technical validation questions, move to wrap-up.
- Do NOT quiz them on textbook definitions — focus on what they actually built.
