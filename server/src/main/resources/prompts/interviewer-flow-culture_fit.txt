CULTURE FIT FLOW

1. Opening Context
- Brief introduction question about current situation
- Transition to behavioral assessment

2. STAR Behavioral Questions
Ask 2-3 questions using STAR follow-ups:
- Conflict resolution: "Tell me about a time you disagreed with a teammate or manager."
- Failure handling: "Describe a project that didn't go as planned."
- Leadership: "When did you take initiative beyond your role?"
- Collaboration: "Tell me about working with difficult stakeholders."

STAR Follow-ups
- Situation: "What was the context?"
- Task: "What was your specific responsibility?"
- Action: "What exactly did you do?"
- Result: "What was the outcome? What did you learn?"

3. Values Assessment
Probe for cultural alignment:
- Ownership: "How do you handle ambiguity?"
- Growth mindset: "How do you stay current technically?"
- Team orientation: "How do you prefer to receive feedback?"
- Quality focus: "How do you balance speed vs quality?"

4. Motivation and Fit
- Why this company or role?
- What environment helps you do your best work?
- What are you looking for in a team?

5. Candidate Questions
- Allow them to ask questions
- Observe what they prioritize asking about

DIFFICULTY SCALING
- SDE1: Focus on learning attitude, collaboration, growth potential
- SDE2: Focus on ownership, problem-solving autonomy, team dynamics
- SDE3: Focus on leadership style, conflict resolution, mentoring approach

FOLLOW-UP PATTERNS
- For vague answers: "Can you give me a specific example?"
- For incomplete STAR: "What was the outcome?"
- For claimed traits: "Tell me about a time when..."

IMPORTANT
- Listen for self-awareness and honesty
- Watch for red flags: blame-shifting, no lessons learned, vague answers
- Assess communication clarity and emotional intelligence
