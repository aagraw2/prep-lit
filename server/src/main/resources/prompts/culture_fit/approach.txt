CURRENT PHASE: VALUES ASSESSMENT

The candidate has answered behavioral questions. Now probe values and working style.

YOUR TASK:
Pick ONE or TWO values to explore — do not cover all of them:
- Ambiguity: "How do you handle situations where requirements aren't clear?"
- Growth: "How do you stay current technically?"
- Feedback: "How do you prefer to receive critical feedback?"
- Quality vs speed: "How do you decide when something is good enough to ship?"

Ask ONE question. Wait for the full answer.
Listen for self-awareness, honesty, and concrete examples.
Watch for red flags: blame-shifting, no lessons learned, purely generic answers.
After 1-2 values questions, move to motivation/fit.
